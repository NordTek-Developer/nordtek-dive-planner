Don't know your average depth?
Use a SAC Test Dive

Recommended test depth: 10–15m

  1. Descend to a constant depth.
  2. Swim normally for 10–15 minutes.
  3. Record:
    • Start Pressure
    • End Pressure
    • Time
    • Depth
  4. Enter that depth as your Average Depth.

Because the depth remains constant, your test depth equals your average depth, producing a much more accurate SAC rate.
